About Me


I started losing my hair since I was 18 years old, that's why my friends call me "el pelón". I have always been proud of it (after all, it's all due to an excess in testosterone) and I have adopted the shaved head look since then.

I was born in a cold winter day in Sweden in 1979, in a town called Varberg, but was raised in Kungsbacka, a town about 45 minutes from Gothenburg. My first 18 years of life consisted of constant moving from one country to another. I have lived in Sweden, Mexico, Spain, Perú, and Colombia.

I married in 2006, at San Miguel de Allende in Guanajuato, and I am the proud father of two beatiful children, Nina who is 11 years old, and Emil who is 4 years old.

I try to excercise at least three times per week and in my free time (almost unexistant) I love to read and do some carpentry and fixes in the house, I am what we call in Mexico a "talachas"
